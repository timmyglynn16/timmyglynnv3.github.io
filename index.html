HI, IM TIMMY

I'm currently pursing a bachelor's degree in computer science with a minor in mathematics at Duquesne University in Pittsburgh, PA. Originally, I came into school as a biomedical engineering major with hopes of working with the latest technology in medicine. However, I developed a very serious interest and passion for computer programming after taking a course in MatLab during my junior year. So I decided to make the decision of changing majors late in the game, knowing it was what I truly wante to pursue in life. Although this move was risky, I haven't looked back since. I love being a CS major and being a contributing member of the software world!

Since entering this major, I have developed programming skills in multiple languages and have learned the in's and out's of programming algorithms and data strucutres. I have worked on projects ranging from simple algorithm implementation, to big data analysis, to real-world problem solving, and more. While it is important to me to exceed in the classroom, nothing is more satisfying than learning more each day and applying my skills in new ways every day. At the end of my college career, I hope to be working in either network security and/or working with big data analysis.

I started this website as not only a way to broaden my skills and teach my self new programming languages, but also to share my passions and interests with other tech nerds out there! So thanks for stopping by, and don't forget to check out all the projects I have to share with you on my site!
